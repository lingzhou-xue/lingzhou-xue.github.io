Overview
My research in machine learning and AI focuses on developing scalable algorithms, optimization methods, and reinforcement learning techniques with applications in health, science, and engineering. These contributions have appeared in leading conferences such as ICML and ICLR, as well as flagship IEEE and INFORMS journals.
